Children's Book Illustrations

Various illustrations for children's books. Some were done based on classic stories / songs, and some are from original stories.

Images created using traditional media and Adobe Photoshop.

  • The Tortoise and the Hare

    My version of the classic Aesop fable featuring the eponymous Tortoise and Hare in automobiles.

  • Little Red Riding Hood - India

    This is Little Red Riding Hood (Shawl) in an Indian jungle followed by a Bengal tiger.

  • Mary Had a Little Lamb

    My attempt to add some levity to the second verse of "Mary Had a Little Lamb." The illustration could serve as a pun if one so desired.
