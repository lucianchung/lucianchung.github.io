NetAgain

NetAgain is a mobile app designed to help people/professionals stay connected with others via a color-coded reminder system. The app features fish-themed illustrations and puns to add levity and fun to networking. Over the course of one month, I designed NetAgain from initial concept to final prototype with guidance and help from user research and senior designers.
